Personal Website Project, Milestone 2

Brief Discription

I decided to construct what I'd imagine a professional portfolio webpage would look like. Based on all of the UI/UX material I've been reading over the past week, I decided not to try to deviate from the popular consensus and attempt to be extremely innovative. Therefore, I decided to keep it rather simple. I did add a little bit of flavor in the wireframe with some things that might be slightly challenging. However, I am absolutely confident that I can make a webpage look exactly as my wireframe does on both the pc/laptop wireframe and the mobile(responsive) wireframe. Below is a list specifying my content strategy for each section of my page.

  • Introduction Section:

    Presents my name, profession, my personalized logo, some quick contact links and a page navigation bar

  • About me section:

    Will give a little bit of a background on my professional standings (then and now type information), what I'm currently investing my time with in programming/development, the direction I'm heading with my education goals and what I'm looking for with my career. This section will also have a professional photograph of myself.

  • Portfolio section:

    This section will provide more insight on my education background, what certifications I've had in the past and currently have, and my relevant professional history. I'm currently starting to work on a few projects/websites that I plan on show casing in this section as well. I'd like to provide a QR link to the capstone project I worked on as well to provide quick access to it if I'm on mobile and want to show off one of my bigger projects.

  • Contact me section:

    This will provide some detailed information on how a person can reach me and provide another link to connect with me now either by phone or email. Also this section will provide a form for a person to input their contact information to send me so that I can reach back out to them.

  • Footer section:

    This section of the page will include icons with embedded links to my professional social media profiles as optional ways to view more information about me or to get in touch with me.
